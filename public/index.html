Tom Keltner

Tom is a resident of Germantown, Ohio. Through the years Tom has sung in church choirs, a high school band, the Centerville Community Chorus, and with 2 higher-profile groups requiring audition entry, The Dayton Philharmonic Chorus and the group Musica! From the Centerville area.
