Tom Keltner Sings the Classics

Tom Keltner

Tom is a resident of Germantown, Ohio. He has always had a keen interest in singing a variety of musical styles from the Beatles to classical to Sinatra. Tom is trained in classical style singing and a former member of the Dayton Philharmonic chorus and the groups Musica and the Centerville Community Chorus. His preference is still the timeless classics from the 1940s, 50s, 60s and contemporary songs that have that sort of feel.
